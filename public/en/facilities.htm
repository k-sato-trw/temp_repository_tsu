Facilities Guide

Reserved Seat B

Admission
  • On-site Race Day:

    ¥500 /person

  • Off-site Race Day:

    ¥500 /person

The reserved seat B area has 764 seats including the pair seats and the single seats that come with tables. A non-smoking section is available. You will enjoy yourself in the comfortable seat.

*Admission fee is to be paid at the entrance of the reserved seat B area.
*No advanced reservation is available.

Reserved Seat A

Admission
  • On-site Race Day:

    ¥2,000 /person

  • Off-site Race Day:

    Closed

This seat offers you the most luxurious and relaxed way to enjoy BOAT RACE Tsu providing a large space around the seat.
The flat TV is set on the desk for your personal viewing of the odds, the live racing and other information. Complimentary drinks and massaging chairs are available in this reserved area.
The non-smoking section is completely separated from the smoking section.

*Admission fee is to be paid at the entrance of the reserved seat A area.
*No advanced reservation is available.
